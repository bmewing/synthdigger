# 13 · Using the Web App

This page is for the **people you share the link with** — family, roommates, friends who
have access to your Plex server. Feel free to copy it to them. No install, no password to
remember.

## Signing in

1. Open the link (the `…ondigitalocean.app` address or your custom domain).
2. Click **Sign in with Plex**. A Plex login opens.
3. Log in with **your own** Plex account and approve.
4. You're in — the page shows "Signed in as *you*".

You can only sign in if your Plex account has access to this server. There's no separate
account to create.

## Making a playlist

Fill in as much or as little as you like, then click **Generate Playlist**:

- **Seed song** — start from a song you like.
- **Freeform idea** — type a vibe, like *"late night drive"*.
- **Genre / Style / Mood** — start typing and pick from suggestions.
- **Base on recent listening** — build from what you've played in the last N days, and
  choose how adventurous ("Similar", "A step away", "Radically different").
- **Number of tracks** — the slider.
- **Advanced options** — fine-tune artist variety, use your own cover image, or set a title.

## Saving to Plex

When you generate a playlist, it's created in **your** Plex account (using your own login) —
so it shows up in Plex on all your devices, ready to play. Your **Your Playlists** section on
the page lists what you've made.

## AI titles and covers

If the owner enabled AI features, you'll see options to auto-generate a catchy title and a
cover image. If you don't see them, they're turned off — everything else works the same.

## Something not working?

- **Can't sign in?** Make sure you're using the Plex account that has access to this server,
  and that you approved the login. Ask the owner to confirm your access.
- **Playlist didn't appear in Plex?** Give it a moment and refresh Plex; if it persists, let
  the owner know (see [[14 Troubleshooting]]).
